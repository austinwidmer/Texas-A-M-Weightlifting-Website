Texas A&M Weightlifting

We are Texas A&M student organization and USAW sanctioned

university weightlifting program. We develop student athletes

through the sport of [olympic] weightlifting, and provide them

the opportunity to represent our school on the national stage.

Originally founded in 2014, the team partnered with

Brazos Valley Barbell to provide a training space for student athletes.

In 2023, the team was reformed after a period of inactivity, and since

then the team has made a strong return to the national level weightlifting scene.
