Donations

The team is appreciative of any and all donations! Your contributions will help fund the team immensely with a variety of things such as:

  • Attending University Nationals
  • Lodging and Transportation for various Meets
  • Developing Student Leaders and Athletes

For sponsorship details, contact txamweightlifting@gmail.com or our Officer Team!

How To Donate

For donations of $250 or more, the Texas A&M Foundation provides an online gift portal which donors can use to donate directly to the team online. In order to make a gift,

  1. Navigate to https://myaggieland.txamfoundation.com/tamf/give
  2. Select an amount to donate, and select "Student Organization Gifts 500-99990."
  3. In the Gift Notes section, enter the organization name "Texas A&M Weightlifting," as well as the account number 955460 to ensure your donation is allocated to our organization.
  4. In the Review Your Selection(s) field, be sure that the designation reads "Student Organization Gifts" and that the amount reflects your intended donation.
    5. Select "Proceed to Billing Details" to complete your donation.

If you would like to recieve a tax receipt for your donation, be sure to email TXAMWeightlifting@gmail.com before donating.
